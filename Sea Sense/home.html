What we do

we're a marine life charity program dedicated to protecting our oceans and the incredible creatures that call them home. We achieve this by spearheading critical initiatives to combat ocean pollution, habitat loss, and threats to marine life. Through these efforts, we aim to ensure a healthier and more sustainable future for our oceans.

How ?

To achieve our goals, we utilize a multifaceted approach that includes on-the-ground cleanups and advocacy campaigns. We also foster collaboration with governments and businesses to create a lasting impact. This allows us to remove plastic pollution directly from our oceans, raise awareness about critical issues, and implement systemic changes for a healthier future.
